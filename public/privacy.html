Neon Puck — Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 17, 2026

Overview

Neon Puck is a free multiplayer air hockey game. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data the app collects — which is essentially nothing.

Data Collection

Neon Puck does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. There are no user accounts, no sign-ins, no analytics, no advertising, and no tracking of any kind.

Online Multiplayer

When you use the online multiplayer feature, the app connects to a game server to relay gameplay data (paddle positions, puck state, and scores) between players in real time. This data is ephemeral — it is not logged or stored. No IP addresses or device identifiers are recorded.

Local Storage

The app does not store any data on your device beyond the app itself. There are no cookies, no local storage, and no saved preferences.

Third-Party Services

Neon Puck does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

Since Neon Puck does not collect any data, it is safe for users of all ages. The app contains no ads, in-app purchases, or external links within the game.
